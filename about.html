What we do ?

We spread awareness among the masses about the various schemes and policies that the government launches for the upliftment of the weaker sections of the society.

How we do this?

Vitamin fortification is one of the effective ways to address deficiencies especially in children and infants. Keeping the issue in mind. Rab da foundation has been providing milk products to children of marginalised communities of Samana district, Patiala for the past one and a half year.

Our Foundation

Our NGO works for providing healthy lifestyle for one's growth and development. We have extended our scope by providing accidental insurances to the peoples across the small tribal areas that are free of costs.
