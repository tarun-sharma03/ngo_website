What we do ?

We spread awareness among the masses about the various schemes and policies launched by state and center government for their benefits. Being aware empowers the people to fight for what is rightfully theirs, thereby uplifting the weaker sections of the society.

How we do this?

Vitamin fortification is one of the effective ways to address deficiencies especially in children and infants. Keeping the issue in mind, Rab Da Banda foundation has been providing milk products to children of marginalised communities for the past one and a half year.

Welcome to Rab Da Banda Foundation

Our NGO works for providing healthy lifestyle for one's growth and development. We have extended our scope by providing accidental insurances to the peoples across the small tribal areas that are free of costs. Moreover, the people are being educated about government schemes and policies like PM suraksha beema yojna and PM Sehat Beema Yojna and enables them to attain this without any hindrances.
